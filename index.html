Peanut Butter and Jelly

Let's learn how to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Pure Peach Jam
  • 2 Slices of Bread

Instructions

  1. Lay out 2 slices of Bread
  2. Spread Peanut Butter on one slice
  3. Spread Jam on the other slice
  4. Stick the two slices together and enjoy!

There are ways to make your PB&J different

  • You could toast the bread first to make a crispy remix of the sandwich
  • Experimenting with other brands of peanut butter and jam will change the taste
  • Have fun while making a new sandwich and don't be afraid of changing things
